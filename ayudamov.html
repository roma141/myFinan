TO CONSIDER

MyFinan totals per month. The date shown defines the month and the date of the entry.

When you want to see another month, change the date and click Update

To modify a entry, go into the details of inputs and outputs and then click Update.
You can update multiple entries at a time

If you select another account and not click on update, the changes you made will not be saved.
